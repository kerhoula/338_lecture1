Trip Overview

About this blog

This site is designed as a personal Dublin travel journal. Each day acts like its own page, with notes about attractions, food, transportation, accessibility, and budget. The goal is to make the trip easy to follow for friends, family, and future students planning a similar experience.

Because accessibility is a major part of my trip, every attraction includes a rating and a short explanation. Budget ratings are included too, because studying abroad can get expensive fast.
